\olsection{Negative Free Logic}

Negative free logic is obtained by supplementing the common rules with
the following rule of \emph{existential committment}, where $R$ is some
predicate, $t$ some term and $a$ !!a{constant}:

\begin{defish}
\AxiomC{\sFmla{\True}{\Atom{F}{\ldots,a,\ldots}}}
\RightLabel{\TRule{}{\lfrexists_{\Log{NFL}}}}
\UnaryInfC{$\sFmla{\True}{\lfrexists a}$}
\DisplayProof
\hfill
\AxiomC{\sFmla{\True}{\eq[a][t]}}
\RightLabel{\TRule{}{\lfrexists_{\Log{NFL}}}}
\UnaryInfC{$\sFmla{\True}{\lfrexists a}$}
\DisplayProof
\end{defish}
