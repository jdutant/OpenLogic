The \Ax{K} rules are given in \olref{natdedpmlk}. Recall that:
\begin{itemize}
    \item In $\Box$\Ax{K} and \Intro{\Diamond}\Ax{K}, the
    sub-!!{derivation} on the right has \emph{at most} 
    \item In $\Box$\Ax{K} the assumptions $\Box!A$ and $\Box!B$ are
    both optional.
    \item In \Intro{\Diamond}\Ax{K} the assumption $\Box!B$ is
    optional but the assumption $\Diamond!A$ is mandatory. 
    \item In systems \Log{D} and stronger, \Intro{\Diamond}\Ax{K}
    can be applied without the assumption $\Diamond!A$.
\end{itemize}
